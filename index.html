Front-End Engineering

The Iron Yard, Las Vegas - June 2017 to October 2017

Wanting a challenge, I looked into learning coding... I started off at the Iron Yard, where I completed a three month intensive immersion course in full stack web development. My classmates and I learned some of the latest concepts in web development and after several weeks of instruction, we were given the opportunity to put our skills into pratice. Assigned to specific projects, we were tasked with building a web application... and later presented the finished product to our peers, instructors, and special guests at a Demo Day. The Ninth Island

July 1, 2012

I was shipped to Las Vegas. Not really wanting to go alone, I brought my cousin along with me. Together, we navigated... I'm kidding! Like everyone else from Hawaii, we had visited Las Vegas many times before we moved here. We were familiar with The Cal, Main Street, and all the other downtown casinos favored by folks from Hawaii, but we had to learn how to navigate the rest of this new town. Most important... we had to figure out where to get a quality chicken katsu plate lunch. We may no longer be proper Kama'aina (Hawaii residents), but Las Vegas has a lot of the comforts of home.
